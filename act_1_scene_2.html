You demand relief.

Justice.

He tells you justice belongs to him.

But he explains his reason for the storm.

Your father once had the ear of the king, and the knowledge of the world at his fingertips.

But kings may turn to cruelty in the name of power.

You were cast to the sea, to be killed or forgotten - whichever came first.

But you're still here, and your father refuses to forget.

The men on the ship are the same from his story.
Now they face his punishment - but he has spared them.

He says he loves you. But is love meant to feel like a storm?

You catch a glimpse of light out of the corner of your eye. So does your father.
